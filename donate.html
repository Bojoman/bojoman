Laptops

Working laptops help students access educational resources and develop digital skills.

Requirements:
  • Windows 7 or newer / macOS 10.10+
  • 4GB RAM minimum
  • Working battery (replaceable)
  • No major physical damage
  • Original charger included
📱

Smartphones

Smartphones provide instant access to information and communication tools.

Requirements:
  • Android 6.0+ or iOS 10+
  • Working screen and buttons
  • Battery holds charge
  • Unlocked or with reset instructions
  • Charger included
📟

Tablets

Tablets are perfect for reading, note-taking, and interactive learning.

Requirements:
  • Android 6.0+ or iOS 10+
  • 10+ inch screen preferred
  • Touch screen fully functional
  • No cracks or dead pixels
  • Charger and case included
